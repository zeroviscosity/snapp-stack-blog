About Me

I'm a full-stack developer working at CrowdRiff in Toronto. This blog is usually technical in nature, although I try to have non-coding posts from time to time. Technology-wise, I'm currently using Scala, Python or Node.js for backend systems, AngularJS and Sass for the frontend, and a mix of SQL and NoSQL databases. As such, my posts will touch on many of those areas. However I love to learn and to learn through doing, so this blog also acts as a log of my experiments.
